My Design Process




Understand

Initial Research

To better understand the problem space, I had conducted primary and secondary research.
The primary research was to understand the users problems and interaction with the existing product.


Whereas, the secondary research was to understand define how other newspaper products and their systems function.

Interview

To better understand my customer target audience, I had conducted interviews with the help of Sanya (member of the Edict). We conducted interviews with the management of The Edict and the students of first, second, third and fourth years of Ashoka University.


The aim of the interviews was to

1) understand problems users encounter while using the current Edict Newspaper. 2) seek advice, and understand goals of the Edict newspaper.

My Learnings

Reflection

  • What I had learnt was that, the key to designing a good experience depended on taking feedback and critics from the users.
  • I also learnt the importance of responsive design as I was struggling to design for different types of device platforms.
  • I learnt that, in this project UX Research, especially usability testing and validation from the management/users is what gave us the confidence and direction as to what type of process to follow.
  • Nevertheless, this was still an MVP in our eyes and in terms of the product features, there was still a lot of features to be explored and a heuristic analysis/usability testing would help us take a better decision in our decision process.
